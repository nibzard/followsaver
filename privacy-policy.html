FollowSaver Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 16, 2025
Privacy First: FollowSaver keeps all your data local. Nothing is sent to external servers or third parties.

What Data We Collect

FollowSaver collects publicly available following and followers data from X/Twitter when you:

  • Visit X/Twitter following or followers pages
  • Scroll through the user lists on these pages

This data includes: usernames, display names, bio information, follower counts, profile images, and account creation dates - all information that is publicly visible on X/Twitter.

How Data Is Stored

All collected data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. This means:

  • Data never leaves your computer
  • No external servers are used
  • No internet connection is required to view your data
  • Only you have access to your collected data

How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely to provide backup and analysis functionality:

  • Display follower/following statistics and ratios
  • Calculate users who don't follow you back
  • Enable CSV and JSON export for personal backup
  • Show collection progress via browser badge notifications

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. All data remains on your local device at all times.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Export: Download your data as CSV or JSON files anytime
  • Delete: Use the "Clear Data" button to remove all collected data
  • Uninstall: Removing the extension automatically deletes all stored data

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally using Chrome's secure storage API, your data is protected by your browser's built-in security measures. No network transmission means no risk of data breaches or interception.

No Tracking

FollowSaver does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Cookies for user identification
  • External advertising networks
  • User behavior monitoring

Updates to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of FollowSaver after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or FollowSaver's data practices, you can:

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Review our source code at github.com/nibzard/followsaver
Open Source: FollowSaver is open source. You can review exactly how your data is handled by examining our code on GitHub.
